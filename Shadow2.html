Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? I've got some answers. Here are the most common questions about working with Shadow.

What services do you offer?

I specialize in web development and logo design. My web development services include custom websites, e-commerce solutions, and responsive design. I also create memorable brand packages.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. Logo design typically takes 3-7 days, while websites can range from 1-4 weeks. I'll provide you with a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

What's your design process look like?

My process starts from understanding your vision and goals. I then create initial concepts, gather your feedback, and refine the design until it's perfect. Throughout the process, I maintain clear communication and provide regular updates on progress.

Do you offer revisions?

Absolutely yes! I include up to 3 rounds of revisions in all my packages to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result. Additional revisions can be accommodated if needed at a reasonable rate.

What are your pricing ranges?

Pricing ranges based on project scope and complexity. Logo design starts from $200, while websites typically range from $800-$5,000+. I offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees and can provide a detailed quote after discussing your specific needs.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! I provide 30 days of free support after project completion for any technical issues or minor adjustments. I also offer ongoing maintenance packages for websites to keep them updated and secure.
